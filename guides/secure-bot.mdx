---
title: "Secure your Bot"
description: "Follow the steps below to reduces the chance of your Bot getting banned."
icon: "shield"
---
<Note>Following these steps doesnt garantee that your bot wont get disabled. Depending on your activity, there will always be a slim chance of your bot getting disabled.</Note>
<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="Do not use your main account!" icon="square-1" color="#ea5a0c">
    Use a clean, un-used account which isnt on any servers to prevent your bot from easily getting disabled.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Only use the bot for RestoreCord." icon="square-2" color="#dc2626">
    Do not use you RestoreCord for any other applications, this gives users the abiity to report your bot for other reasons than RestoreCord.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Do not sell Members on RestoreCord." icon="square-3" color="#dc2626">
    Do not sell members on RestoreCord, this is against Discords Terms Of Service and will result in your bot getting disabled.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Use a custom domain." icon="square-4" color="#16a34a">
    Using a custom domain completely hides the RestoreCord brand on Discords side. This makes it harder for Discord an and your members to detect that you are using RestoreCord.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Use an appropriate bot name." icon="square-5" color="#dc2626">
    Use a name that is appropriate for your bot. This will make it harder for Discord to detect that you are using RestoreCord. Do not impersonate other bots.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Add the bot to an application team." icon="square-6" color="#0285c7">
    By adding your bot to an application team, you can lower your chance of loosing access to the bot.  .
  </Card>

</CardGroup>
